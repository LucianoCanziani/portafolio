e-Presis is a comprehensive logistics management system designed for Courier, Logistics, and Ecommerce companies. It provides end-to-end solutions for managing shipments, tracking, and integration with various marketplaces.

## Key Features

- **Integral Logistics Management:** Specialized for national and international courier services, mailroom administration, and motorcycle delivery services.
- **Ecommerce Integration:** Seamlessly integrates with all major e-commerce platforms and marketplaces in minutes.
- **Custom Development:** Tailored software adjustments to adapt the system to each client's specific business needs.
- **Implementation Support:** Full guidance through all stages of the implementation process.
- **Online Tracking:** Real-time tracking capabilities for logistics and courier operations.
